# Before posting any job  
If you want to post a job on our platform, please read **carefully**. Job posts that do not follow the rules will be rejected.

# Vietnam Startup Job Board

A curated job platform connecting top talent with Vietnam’s most innovative startups.  
We focus on quality, transparency, and meaningful opportunities. Our mission is simple: **help great companies hire better, and help great people find the right place to grow.**

If you are hiring, please note that we strongly believe **good companies provide clear, complete, and respectful job descriptions**. To maintain quality for candidates, all employers must follow the guidelines below.  
Job posts that don’t meet the requirements may be removed.

---

## What types of jobs can be posted?

We accept job posts related to:

- Software Engineering (all stacks)  
- Product Management  
- UI/UX & Design  
- Data Engineering / Analytics / AI / ML  
- DevOps / SRE / Cloud / Cybersecurity  
- Mobile Engineering (Android/iOS)  
- QA / Automation  
- Startup business roles (Marketing, Growth, Ops, Customer Success)  
- Other technical or startup-related positions  

If your role is relevant to the Vietnam startup ecosystem, it is welcome here.

---

## How to submit a new job post?

1. Create a job post following the required format.  
2. Submit it via our platform (or by opening a GitHub issue if you prefer Git-based submission).  
3. Your post will be reviewed and published once approved.

---

## How long will a job post stay published?

Job posts stay live for **90 days**. Expired posts will be archived automatically.  
You may refresh or re-submit anytime.

---

# Required Format for Job Posts

All job posts **must be written in English** and follow the structure below.  
Markdown format is required. If you’re new to Markdown, see this guide: <http://commonmark.org/help/tutorial/>

---

## Job Title Format

```
[Company Name] - [Job Title] - [Location] - [Job Type]
```

**Job Type options:**
- FT (Full-time)
- PT (Part-time)
- C (Contract)
- Intern

**Example:**

```
Hanoian Labs - Backend Engineer (Go) - Remote/HCMC - FT
```

❌ Titles cannot be in ALL CAPS  
❌ No special formatting: `[Senior] Engineer`  
❌ No salesy content: “$4k salary - HOT JOB”
❌ No emoji in the title

---

# Job Description Template

Please use the following structure:

```
## Location (REQUIRED)
- Saigon / Hanoi / Remote / Hybrid

## Salary Range (REQUIRED)
- $1500–$3000/month  
- Or state “competitive” if the salary cannot be disclosed

## About the Company
Provide a concise introduction to your startup, covering:
- What your company does  
- Your mission and vision  
- The product or problem you are solving  

## Requirements (REQUIRED)
List the essential qualifications for this role, including:
- Required technical skills  
- Necessary experience levels  
- Relevant non-technical expectations (communication, ownership, etc.)

## Skills

### Must Have (REQUIRED)
- Core technical skills required for the role  
- Mandatory experience areas or tools  

### Nice to Have
- Additional skills or experience that would be beneficial  

## Responsibilities (REQUIRED)
Clearly describe:
- Day-to-day responsibilities  
- Areas of ownership  
- Scope of work and team collaboration  

## Why You’ll Love Working Here (REQUIRED)
Highlight what makes your company a great place to work:
- Culture and values  
- Benefits and perks  
- Learning and career growth opportunities  
- Meaningful impact the candidate can make  

## Interesting Projects (OPTIONAL but recommended)
Share examples of:
- High-impact engineering challenges  
- Notable product or technical initiatives  
- Unique problems your team is solving  

## Contact (REQUIRED)
- Company website or homepage  
- Contact email for applications (phone number optional)
```

---

## Checklist Before Submission

Before submitting, please ensure:

- Your post is written in clear English  
- You follow the required title format  
- Your job description uses Markdown  
- All REQUIRED sections are filled  
- Salary or salary range is included (or “competitive”)  
- Grammar and spelling are checked  

Job posts that do not follow the guidelines may be rejected or removed.
